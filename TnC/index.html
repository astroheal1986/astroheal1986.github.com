1. SERVICE OVERVIEW
The App is part of a service designed to enable registered users to Geo Tag location of the self or people nearby with photo of location and type of Geo Tag such as Quarantine or tested Positive for the severe acute respiratory syndrome Coronavirus2(Covid-19). The Geo Tags created by tapping on the Map screen will be visible to any registered user within nearby area. When the app is installed it makes use of the GPS of the mobile device to send the current location to the server and then fetch nearby Geo Tags which are shown in different colours. Tapping on the Geo Tags will take the registered user to the Tag Detail screen where photo and name submitted by other user will be visible.
The purpose of the app is to fight Covid-19 with making people aware of the people who have been put in Quarantine or tested Positive. We keep email address of the user who submits the Geo Tag safely on our servers, other than that we don't track any other information. We do store the Tag information which has the Location coordinates, Photo submitted by the user and the Time of submission.

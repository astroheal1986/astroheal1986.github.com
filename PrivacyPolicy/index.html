This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time so please re-visit this page from time to time. We will try to notify you but that cannot be guaranteed. In order to use the App, you will be required to consent to the terms of the Privacy Policy as revised from time to time.

1. INFORMATION COLLECTED AND MANNER OF COLLECTION
a. When you Sign-in with Google on the App, the following information is collected from your Google account and stored securely on the cloud - (i) Name; (ii) Email.
b. When you Geo Tag a location with the Name of that person, photo and type of Tag all of this information along with the Location coordinates are saved safely in our databases on cloud.
2. USE OF INFORMATION
a. The personal information collected from or about you under Clause 1(a) above, will be stored locally in the App on your device and will only be uploaded to and used by creator of the App(astroheal1986@gmail.com) (i) in anonymised, aggregated datasets for the purpose of generating reports and/or (ii) identifying the user who created Geo Tag in order to prevent inaccurate Geo Tags being created. Any personal information collected and uploaded to cloud may also be shared with Indian Government or any such Administrative body in order to carry out necessary medical and administrative interventions.
b. The email and name collected from signing up with Google may be used to communicate with you through emails, push notification or other such means to inform you about the updated pertaining to COVID-19 as may be required.
c. The personal information collected will not be used for any purpose other than those mentioned in the Clause 2 as required in order to comply with a legal requirement.
3. RETENTION

All information provided at the time of registration will be retained for as long as your account remains in existence and for such period thereafter as required for the purposes for which the information may lawfully be used or is otherwise required under any other law for the time being in force.
b. All the information provided while creating the Geo Tag will be retained for as long as your account remains in existence and for such period thereafter as required for the purposes for which the information may lawfully be used or is otherwise required under any other law for the time being in force.

b. You cannot manage the communications that you receive from us or how you receive them. If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, you may log out at any time.
5. DATA SECURITY
The App is equipped with standard security features to protect the confidentiality and security of your information. Data is encrypted in transit as well as at rest. Personal information provided at the time of registration is encrypted before being uploaded to the cloud where it is stored in a secure encrypted server.
